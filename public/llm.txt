# ContentAI — AI Content Generation in 5 Languages
# https://contentai.ca

ContentAI is an AI-powered content generation platform that creates native-quality content in English, French, Arabic, Spanish, and Chinese. It is designed for businesses serving MENA, francophone, and global multilingual markets.

## What ContentAI does
- Generates blog posts, social media captions, email campaigns, and ad copy
- Supports 5 languages natively (EN, FR, AR, ES, 中文) — not translation-based
- Full RTL (right-to-left) support for Arabic
- Auto-posts to Twitter, LinkedIn, and Facebook after one-tap approval
- Brand voice profiles ensure consistent tone across all content
- Seasonal and cultural content awareness (Ramadan, Lunar New Year, regional holidays)
- Built on Claude AI (Anthropic)

## Pricing
- Free: 5 generations/month
